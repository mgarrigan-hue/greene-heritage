# A3 — Catherine Clarke marriage (1875-1885, Co. Meath)

**Query:** type=marriage, firstname=Catherine, lastname=Clarke, year 1875-1885, location "Co. Meath"
**Result:** 23 total hits (top 10 captured), no DB error.

## Important interpretation
The goal is to find a Catherine who **married a Clarke male** (so her son's grandfather is the Clarke father-of-husband). The IG marriage index returns Catherine where Clarke appears as either spouse. Of the top 10:

- **Rows 1-7**: Catherine *née* Clarke marrying various men → NOT the target.
- **Rows 8-10**: Catherine marrying INTO Clarke → **THESE ARE THE TARGETS.**
