This page is the visual companion to Sources & Citations. Where Sources lists every record by category, Gallery shows you the actual scans, registers, and contextual imagery — grouped by person and place, with full credits.

Primary evidence badges mark scans of original civil registers, census forms, and church records — the documents that anchor specific factual claims. Context badges mark period and place imagery (public-domain photographs, historic illustrations) that help visualise the world the family lived in.

The catalogue is generated from research-results/gallery-inventory.json. Click any image to view it full-size with caption, credit, licence, and link to original source.
